Questions from people with a lot going on

Is this anonymous?

That’s the goal. Keep names, identifying details, and anything genuinely risky out of the machine.

Is this therapy?

Absolutely not. It’s a tiny internet ritual for closure, comedy, and emotional spring cleaning.

What kinds of things should I shred?

Regrets, cringe memories, texts you shouldn’t have sent, social disasters, petty thoughts, and any other harmless little ghosts.

What kinds of things should not go in here?

Real names, identifying details, threats, illegal stuff, revenge content, or anything that could hurt an actual person.
