how it works

One tiny act of delightfully needless closure

This is not therapy, penance, or a life plan. It’s a short, stylish send-off for harmless social debris and low-stakes regret.

1. Type the incident

Late-night text. Meeting blunder. Weird interaction. The thing your brain keeps replaying for sport.

2. Choose the spectacle

Go clean and corporate, cut it into tiny squares, burn it with ceremony, or launch it into tasteful oblivion.

3. Feel better than delete ever managed

You are not here for productivity. You are here for theatrical emotional housekeeping.

faq

Reasonable questions about an unreasonable product

Is this anonymous?

That’s the goal. Keep real names and identifying details out of the machine and you’ll be using it as intended.

Is this therapy?

No. It’s a stylish joke product with an oddly satisfying little ritual attached.

What belongs in Secret Shredder?

Harmless regrets, low-stakes cringe, texts you wish could be unsent, petty thoughts you should never publish, and similar social debris.

What definitely does not belong in here?

Real names, identifying details, threats, illegal material, revenge content, or anything meant to harm a real person.
