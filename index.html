faq

Questions from people with a lot going on

Is this anonymous?

That’s the point. The experience should collect as little as possible, avoid unnecessary storage, and never ask for real names inside the ritual.

Why not just use Stripe Payment Links?

They’re fast, but the Stripe API gives better control over checkout, analytics, offers, post-payment flows, and future premium packs from day one.

Can this run well behind Cloudflare?

Yes. Static front-end on Pages, API endpoints in Workers, environment secrets in Cloudflare, and SMTP notifications for launch capture is a clean fit.

What still needs wiring?

Stripe keys, checkout endpoint, Breevo SMTP credentials, the destination email inbox, and any lightweight moderation rules or analytics you want in production.
