Privacy

Secret Shredder is built to keep things light, anonymous, and low-drama. We only want the minimum information needed to run the site, process payments, and let people know when something new is happening.

If you join the list, we may collect your email address and the details you choose to submit. If you buy a shred, payment information is handled by our payment provider rather than stored directly by us.

Please do not include real names, contact details, or identifying information in anything you submit. This is meant to be a silly little ritual for harmless regrets, not a place to publish personal information.

We may use trusted service providers to help us host the site, process payments, and send launch updates. As the product evolves, this page may be updated to reflect how the service operates.

If you have privacy questions, use the contact details provided when the service officially opens.
