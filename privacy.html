Privacy

Secret Shredder is intended to collect as little personal information as practical for a novelty paid ritual. This page is a launch-stage draft and still needs final legal review before public production use.

If you join the launch list, we may collect your email address, optional category selections, and basic technical information needed to operate the site safely. If you purchase a shred, payments should be handled by Stripe, and we should avoid storing unnecessary payment data ourselves.

Confessions submitted through the ritual should be treated as transient product content, not long-term profile data. The production implementation should minimise retention, discourage identifying details, and avoid storing secrets longer than operationally necessary.

We may use service providers such as Cloudflare, Stripe, and Breevo (or another SMTP provider) to operate hosting, payments, and launch communications.

Before public launch, this page should be replaced or reviewed by a qualified legal professional with the exact data flow, retention policy, refund policy, and content moderation process in mind.
