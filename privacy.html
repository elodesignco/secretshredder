Privacy

Secret Shredder is meant to be a low-drama, low-data product. We only want the minimum information needed to run the site, handle payments if they exist, and contact you if you deliberately join the list.

If you sign up for updates, we may collect your email address and anything else you choose to submit in that form. If paid features exist, payment details are handled by the payment provider rather than stored directly by us.

Please do not put real names, contact details, or identifying information into anything you submit. Secret Shredder is for harmless cringe and small regrets — not personal data dumps.

We may use trusted providers for hosting, email delivery, analytics, and payments.

If the product changes, this page may change with it.
